Trindle store

We stock a range of specialty grocery items, garden accessories and fun, unique gifts for kids, gardeners and four legged friends. Whether you're planning a lavish dinner, hunting for hard-to-find ingredients, or simply indulging in gourmet goodness, you're sure to find something amongst our imported cheeses, aged meats, handcrafted chutneys and other locally sourced items.

Garden centre

From seasonal flowers and decorative shrubs to fruit trees and houseplants our garden centre offers a multitude of options to provide color for your garden now and in the coming seasons. We also stock tools, compost, pots, and everything you need to tend plant life. Our expert gardeners are on hand to provide horticulture tips, planting guides and landscaping advice.

Coffee Shed

Combining our amazing espresso with plenty of hot, foamy milk, our lattes are Kew village's favourite, not to mention our iced lattes, flat whites and matcha teas. We offer an array of home baked goods, both sweet and savoury, made with local produce . Enjoy your morning coffee or afternoon pick me up surrounded by plants and shrubs in our garden centre space.

Events

Our garden space provides the ideal environment for outdoor events - weddings, anniversaries, reunions and any kind of get together. Past events have included outdoor screening of Wimbledon tennis, 50th birthday party and a charity garden party for children. Contact us at hello@trindle-stores.com for ideas.

About us

We are a local family-run business and have planned this venture for years. After a period of learning about wholesale accounts, sourcing products, and juggling the demands of running a business alongside family life, we opened in November 2024. Our garden center and grocery store are filled with "magical delights" We aim to foster community by offering a relaxed atmosphere where people can connect, enjoy coffee, and browse a selection of goods like olive oil and plants. Come in and say hi!
